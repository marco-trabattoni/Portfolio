Il Gesto Eterno
TEAM PROJECT
Branding, Layout Design, UI Design, Coding
Il Gesto Eterno is a project with the aim of making accessible three Lucio Fontana's paintings housed within the Museo del 900' in Milan.

In line with this objective, scale reproductions of Fontana's triptych have been created; the materiality and depth of the cuts reproduced on the canvases allows people with visual impairments to tactilely enjoy the artworks.

A printed brochure has also been designed to provide a tactile "experience" to be enjoyed individually. Through the QR code it's then possible to access an audio player, specifically designed to be easily used by blind people.
The audio player implements a non-visual interactive interface, taking advantage of the entire screen of the mobile device to manage user interactions without the need to see what's happening on the screen.
