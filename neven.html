Neven Generative Identity
TEAM PROJECT
Branding, Creative Coding, UI Design
For the Neven project, a graphic artifact generator was designed to allow customization based on specific parameters (developed using p5.js).

Neven is a fictional electronic music festival that celebrates the connection between music and nature, using water as a symbol of this relationship. Set near a body of water, the festival focuses on IDM music, a genre known for its synthetic yet emotionally rich soundscapes.

The aim of the festival is to merge the synthetic, digital nature of this genre with the organic, emotional connection to nature that the music seeks to evoke. To visually represent this concept, the identity is based on the overlap of two contrasting visual languages. The background of the artworks is built through compositions made of ASCII characters, evoking a coded and artificial landscape.

On top of this layer, the typography undergoes a pixelation process that evolves over time: the closer we get to the date of the event, the more fragmented and dissolved the letterforms appear.
The progressive pixelation reflects the emotional and sensory experience that the music aims to stimulate, while the motion of the ASCII composition mimics the behavior and visual traits of water.
